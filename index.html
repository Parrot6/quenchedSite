Awesome Features


Quenched was founded on the idea that finding the right craft brewery for tonight should not be hard. In reality though, finding a brewery that fits your needs can be difficult with information spread out among each brewery’s website, Facebook, and Instagram. That is where we come in, with our database of over 9000 breweries in the US combined with our Quenched Filters, finding your next brewery experience is just a few taps away.

Every Brewery in one place

No more endless searching breweries online to find one that looks interesting. Quenched has a massive database of breweries and is adding new ones all the time. You can search by state, town, zip code, or let us show you what is around you right now.

Quenched Filters

On vacation and looking for a great brewery experience? Looking for a place to meet friends, but not sure where to go? Quenched lets you search all the breweries in your area and filter them using our Descriptors so you can find the right brewery.

Share your experience

Love the Craft Brewery experience as much as we do?! Quenched lets you upload photos of your brewery adventures to our Brewery Experience Page. You might even have your photo selected to be part of your favorite brewery’s Quenched profile.

Help your favorite breweries

Can’t find your favorite brewery on Quenched, or if you know of a new brewery in town, help them out by suggesting a new brewery. You can also help make Quenched better by recommending Descriptors for your favorite breweries while using the App.
